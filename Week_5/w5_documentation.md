# Week 5 Tutorial 5 Documentation

## 1. Answer Questions

### 1.1 Define the problem statement

A café needs a program that can automatically calculate customer bills based on the quantity of items purchased.

### 1.2 What are the inputs?

The inputs are:
- Customer name
- Quantity of coffee
- Quantity of tea
- Quantity of sandwich

### 1.3 What are the outputs?

The outputs are:
- Customer receipt
- Purchased items and quantities
- Total bill amount

### 1.4 What would be the typical process flow?

1. Get customer information.
2. Get quantity of items purchased.
3. Calculate the total price.
4. Generate and display the receipt.

### 1.5 What are the constraints?

- Only available items can be ordered.
- Prices are fixed.
- Quantity must be a valid number.

---

## 2. Problem Decomposition

The problem can be divided into smaller tasks:

1. Store item prices.
2. Get customer input.
3. Calculate coffee cost.
4. Calculate tea cost.
5. Calculate sandwich cost.
6. Calculate total bill.
7. Print receipt.
